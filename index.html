Reach For Unlimited Productivity

Personal Project Productivity Manager (3PM) is the essential tool for the modern knowledge worker.

Find Out Why

Trying to maintain uninterrupted concentration and getting lost in unimportant details is bad for your productivity. Furthermore, research shows that sitting for too long is bad for your health (back, metabolism, heart, and brain) and significantly increases your risk of heart disease, diabetes, and premature death.

The Pomodoro Technique is a scientifically proven methodology to increase productivity invented by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. It is a cyclical system, where you work in short sprints (25 minutes are recommended) interrupted by short breaks (5 minutes - get up!). Before starting a sprint, you choose a task to work on exclusively. If you are distracted during a sprint, you postpone the issue until after the sprint or - if the issue requires immediate attention - end the sprint. Every 4 sprints, you end the session and take a longer break (15-30 minutes). During this time, your brain will process recent information and prepare for the next session of 4 sprints.

3PM is an advanced productivity management app based on the pomodoro technique. 3PM helps you pack more work into less time...

...so you, too, can leave the office every day by 3 p.m.!

Find Out How

How 3PM helps you unleash your productivity:

Crystal Clear Focus

3PM uses advanced psychological methods to reinforce your incentive to focus exclusively on the task at hand. You will maintain a state of deep concentration throughout the workday.

Deliver on Time

3PM learns your work habits and predicts how much time you need to finish a task, using a sophisticated statistical algorithm called bootstrapping. You will never miss a deadline again!
